
# bandit hideout

the bandit hideout is a makeshift base set in a forest

## enemies

various simple bandit enemies with different weapons and stats
- dagger bandit
- sword bandit
- twinsword bandit
- bow bandit
- bandit boss

## environmental

- kickable barrels
- smashable barrels
- arrow traps

## loot

weapons
- steel sword
- steel twinswords
- steel dagger
- reinforced bow

armor
- steel breastplate
- chainmail

consumables
- dart

other
